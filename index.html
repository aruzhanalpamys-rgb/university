Business Essentials

Interpret data to inform business decisions and understand financial foundations.

CORe Program

This program introduces learners to core business concepts including economics, accounting, and business analytics. You will analyze real-world cases, interpret financial statements, and make data-driven decisions.

  • Duration: 10 weeks
  • Effort: 10–12 hrs/week
  • Credential: Certificate
  • Format: Online
  • Price: $2,650

Business Analytics

Learn how to use data analysis tools to support business strategy and operational decision-making. Covers regression, forecasting, and visualization.

  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Effort: 8–10 hrs/week
  • Skill level: Beginner
  • Projects included
  • Certificate awarded
